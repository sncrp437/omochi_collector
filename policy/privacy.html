Privacy Policy

Serendipity Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") establishes the following privacy policy regarding the handling of users' personal information in the service "Omochi" (hereinafter referred to as "this Service") provided by the Company.

1. Information We Collect

When users use this service, the Company may collect the following information:

  • Name, address, phone number, email address
  • Reservation information, order history
  • Device information, IP address, browser information
  • Cookie information, access logs

2. Purpose of Use

The collected personal information will be used for the following purposes:

  • Service provision, identity verification, reservation/order processing, billing for reservation fees and service usage fees
  • Support and inquiry response
  • Service improvement and new feature development
  • Prevention of unauthorized use, security assurance

3. Third-Party Provision

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • When there is consent from the individual
  • When required by law
  • When providing to payment processors within the necessary scope for billing reservation fees and service usage fees
  • When providing to partner stores where users have made reservation applications
  • When providing to business partners within the necessary scope

4. Security Measures

The Company will implement necessary and appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, leakage, alteration, loss, etc. of personal information.

5. Use of Cookies

This service may use cookies to improve user convenience and for access analysis.

6. Integration with External Services

We may integrate with external services such as Google Analytics. Please check each company's privacy policy when using these services.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

The Company may change the content of this policy without notice. Changes will be notified on this page.

8. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding this policy, security measures, and procedures for disclosure of retained personal data, please contact us below.

Serendipity Co., Ltd.
Email: info@omochiapp.com
